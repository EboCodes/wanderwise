Practical advice

Things we wish we'd known earlier

The guides that save you time, money and the specific frustration of discovering something the hard way at the worst possible moment.

Browse all tips →
01

Book trains in Europe at least 3 weeks out

Advance fares on Eurostar and Trenitalia can be 60-70% cheaper than walk-up prices.

02

US National Parks: go mid-week, arrive early

The famous spots are genuinely different at 7am before the tour buses arrive. Worth the alarm.

03

Carry a physical backup of key bookings

Hotel vouchers, train reservations, car rental numbers. One dead phone derails more trips than bad weather.

04

Learn ten words of the local language

Hello, thank you, please, excuse me, and do you have a table. Opens more doors than a pocket phrasebook ever will.
